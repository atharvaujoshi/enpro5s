# Product Guidelines - Zone Photo Tracker

## Brand Personality
- **Modern and Clean:** The application focuses on ease of use and clarity, providing a modern interface that simplifies the documentation process for industrial environments.

## Communication Style (Prose)
- **Supportive and Helpful:** The tone is guiding and encouraging, ensuring users feel supported throughout the photo capture and reporting workflow. Instructions and feedback should be clear but approachable.

## Visual Identity & UX
- **High Contrast:** UI elements are designed for maximum visibility, accommodating users working in varying lighting conditions often found in industrial sites.
- **Soft and Balanced:** While maintaining high contrast, the overall aesthetic remains balanced and comfortable for long-term use.
